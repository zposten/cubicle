## Ingredients

| Ingredients          | Amount   |
| -------------------- | -------- |
| Bisquick pancake mix | 1 cup    |
| sugar                | 1.5 tbsp |
| baking powder        | 2tsp     |
| Milk                 | 1/2 cup  |
| Vegetable oil        | 1 tbsp   |
| Eggs                 | 1        |
| Vanilla              | 1/2 tsp  |

## Make It

1. Combine all ingredients in a medium sized mixing bowl
1. Cook pancakes!!
